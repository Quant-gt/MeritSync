The USA job market is brutal right now

If you were just laid off — or you've searched for months with nothing to show — here's the honest reason the job search is so hard in 2026.

Your Résumé Never Gets Seen by Humans

75% of résumés are rejected by Applicant Tracking Systems before a human recruiter reads them. One fancy template or misplaced section and you're auto-rejected — even if you're perfectly qualified.

02
500:1
Apps per Interview

500+ Applications = 0 Interviews

You apply. Nothing happens. You apply again. Still nothing. Your materials aren't optimized, your LinkedIn profile isn't discoverable, and your outreach templates are generic.

6–8 Interview Rounds (And You're Not Ready)

Phone screen → take home test → technical assessment → behavioral panel → manager sync → executive round. You could interview for weeks, and rustiness costs you the offer at round 2 or 3.

Being laid off isn't just lost income. It's replaying the HR call, the anxiety, the imposter syndrome. A better résumé won't help if you're paralyzed by self-doubt during high-stakes pitches.
